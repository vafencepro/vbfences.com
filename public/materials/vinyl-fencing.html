Vinyl fence installation in Virginia Beach and Hampton Roads

Vinyl fencing is the low-maintenance alternative to wood — no painting, no staining, no rot. VB Fences installs vinyl privacy, semi-privacy, and picket fences across Virginia Beach, Norfolk, Chesapeake, Portsmouth, and Suffolk.

Privacy and semi-privacy panels

Full privacy and alternating louver panels in white, tan, and gray. UV-stabilized vinyl that holds color in coastal sun.

Low maintenance design

No painting, no sealing, no annual treatment. Wash with a hose. Vinyl is especially well-suited to Virginia Beach's salt-air environment.

Pool and pet applications

Vinyl meets Virginia pool barrier code and holds up to pet contact without splintering or warping.
