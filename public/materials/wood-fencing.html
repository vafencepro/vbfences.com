Wood fence installation in Virginia Beach and Hampton Roads

Wood fencing offers natural privacy, strong curb appeal, and flexibility in design that vinyl and metal can't match. VB Fences installs pressure-treated wood privacy fences, shadowbox fences, and framed gates across Hampton Roads.

Privacy and shadowbox styles

Solid board-on-board and alternating shadowbox panels from 4 to 8 feet. Ideal for backyard enclosure and wind protection.

Pressure-treated construction

All wood components are pressure-treated for Virginia's humid climate. Post bases set in concrete to resist shifting and rot.

Custom gates

Single and double swing gates in matching wood. Heavy-duty hinges and drop rods standard on double gates.
