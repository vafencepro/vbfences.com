Aluminum fence installation in Virginia Beach and Hampton Roads

Aluminum fencing combines a finished, professional look with zero maintenance and long-term corrosion resistance — ideal for Hampton Roads' coastal climate. VB Fences installs aluminum perimeter and pool fences across all five service cities.

Pool code compliance

Aluminum is the material of choice for Virginia pool barrier requirements. Panels sized to code, self-closing gates standard.

Corrosion resistance

Powder-coated aluminum does not rust in salt air. Ideal for Virginia Beach and Chesapeake properties near water.

Decorative and perimeter styles

Flat-top and spear-top aluminum in black and bronze. Clean sight lines for front yards, commercial perimeters, and community entrances.
