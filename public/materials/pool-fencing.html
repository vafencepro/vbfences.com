Pool Fencing FAQs

Does VB Fences install pool fences?

Yes. Pool fencing is one of our core specialties, built to meet Virginia residential pool barrier requirements for panel height, spacing, and gate hardware.

What do Virginia's pool fence rules generally require?

Pool barriers generally need adequate height, limited gaps or climbable footholds, and gates that self-close and self-latch above a child's reach. We confirm the exact requirement for your property and jurisdiction before installing.

What material do you recommend for a pool fence?

Powder-coated aluminum is our standard pool fence material. It resists rust in Hampton Roads' salt air, needs no repainting or sealing, and is available in flat-top or spear-top styles.

Can you add a pool fence to an existing yard fence?

Yes. We regularly tie a code-compliant pool enclosure into an existing wood, vinyl, or aluminum perimeter fence.

Do you install pool fencing outside Virginia Beach?

Yes. VB Fences installs pool fencing across Virginia Beach, Norfolk, Chesapeake, Portsmouth, and Suffolk.
