Chain link fence installation in Virginia Beach and Hampton Roads

Chain link is the most cost-effective perimeter solution for residential backyards, commercial lots, and utility enclosures. VB Fences installs galvanized and vinyl-coated chain link across Virginia Beach, Norfolk, Chesapeake, Portsmouth, and Suffolk.

Residential and commercial grades

11.5-gauge residential and 9-gauge commercial fabric available. Post sizing matched to fence height and application.

Galvanized and vinyl-coated

Standard galvanized for utility and commercial use. Vinyl-coated black and green for residential backyards where appearance matters.

Gates and access control

Single swing, double swing, and roll gates. Padlock hardware standard. Slide gates available for vehicle access on commercial lots.
